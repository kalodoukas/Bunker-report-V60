Technical notes

  • Fuel tank list includes the selected BY-7 fuel tanks plus OVF / H.F.O. OVERFLOW T. (P). In v45 the calibration and heel correction tables supplied in BY7_tables_original_layout_manual_verified.xlsx are treated as Verified by manual review.
  • Export Template XLS produces the company template-style forms based on the uploaded workbook: 015 BP, 026 BFR and 026 AFT.
  • All selected tanks are always listed, including OVF. Tanks without measurements are exported with zero quantities. The latest saved measurement per tank/stage is used.
  • The export includes S% / Sulphur %, product totals by HFO, LS FO, ULS FO, MGO, ULS MGO and DO, and A4 landscape print settings fitted to one page per sheet.
  • For ullage entries, the app converts: Sounding = Reference height − Ullage. Reference height for this tank: 1858.7 cm.
  • Trim is interpolated between calibration columns −4.0 m by stern to +2.0 m by stem. Heel/List correction is applied directly for NO. 1 H.F.O. T. (S). For NO. 1 H.F.O. T. (P), v17 uses a mirrored candidate of the sister tank correction. For NO. 2 H.F.O. T. (P/S), v17 starts provisional heel correction candidates based on the located NO.2 heel correction pages; these must be visually verified before operational use.
  • Default VCF is an API/ASTM 54B-style calculated CTL estimate. For bunker-survey custody work, use Manual VCF override with the VCF from API/Dens or approved survey software.
